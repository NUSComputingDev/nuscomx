21st Management Committee Elections

Have you participated in previous Computing Club activities, and experienced first hand the great synergy running through the organisers, as well as with the participants? Do you see great experience, potential and passion in yourself, which can be put into use to further the cause of your fellow faculty-mates?

As students of SoC, we have reaped much benefits from the efforts of the previous Computing Club Management Committees. Heed the calling to contribute, to serveand to lead. Step up, present yourself to your fellow students, and take up the challenge as the NUS Students' Computing Club 21st Management Committee.
